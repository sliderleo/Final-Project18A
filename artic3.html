TNC Tigers Announced as New Dota 2 Sister Team for TNC Pro

By Brandon Brathwaite 05 MAR 2018

TNC Pro Team will have a new sister team for Dota 2 led by the team's former captain Sivatheeban "1437" Sivanathapillai. The new team called TNC Tiger will be a SEA based team with an international roster, according to 1437 who confirmed the information via Reddit. The roster will be announced on the TNC tigers page the upcoming days. 1437 ended his time with TNC Pro Team in January after eight months with the team. They finished 9th-12th at The International 7 and were able to win at China Top before the end of the year.

1437 shared his gratitude for the fans that kept him going through the process stating, "Thank you so much guys for the support. It's been a rough month especially with how things ended at TNC Phoenix and putting together this new team but I'm really looking forward to the brighter future. My dedication and passion to this game has never been stronger and it's all because you guys have been absolutely amazing supporters and keep me going. There are still many goals I wish to achieve and prove in Dota 2. So here's to a new beginning. Thank you all again."
