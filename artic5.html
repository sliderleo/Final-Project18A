Virtus.pro Win the Bucharest Major in Dominating Fashion

By Brandon Brathwaite 11 MAR 2018

​​Virtus.Pro are the new Bucharest Major champions! In dominating fashion, VP sweeped the finals with a 3-0 victory over VGJ. Thunder.

Check out the @virtuspro Winners' Ceremony. The moment we have been building towards for months... #thebucharestmajor https://t.co/hc6DynNgPE

— PGL (@pglesports) March 11, 2018

​​Virtus.pro ​capped off qualifying for the International 8 with a win here at the Bucharest Major, bringing their overall Major tournament wins in the Pro Circuit to three.

Virtus.pro dropped the opening match of the Bucharest Major to OpTic Gaming during the group stages of the event. The team went on to defeat LGD Forever Young, Natus Vincere, and TNC Pro Team to get out of the group stages with a 3-1 record.

In the playoffs, Virtus.pro had its rematch against OpTic Gaming, and the team got a 2-0 revenge victory. Virtus.pro faced off against the current International champions, Team Liquid, in a close 2-1 match that sent Virtus.pro to the grand final against VGJ. Thunder.

VGJ. Thunder were undefeated in the tournament during the group stages, and cruised to the grand final. VP ultimately stopped the VGJ. Thunder run to secure the tournament victory.
