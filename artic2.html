Dendi Wins MOBA Gamer of the Year at French Awards Presentation

By Brandon Brathwaite 27 Feb 2018

Danil "Dendi" Ishutin, Dota 2 player for Natus Vincere, won the "Best MOBA Gamer of the Year" award from the Great Gamers Awards in France.

The long-time Na'Vi player had one Major championship win at Adrenaline Cyber League and multiple top finishes at events like DreamLeague Season 8 Finals and PGL Open Bucharest. Dendi broke the record for most games played with one team in December 2015 and has played 1,421 games with Na'Vi.

The Great Gamers Awards awards featured categories in first person shooters, real-time strategy, sports (featuring all fighting game players) and entertainer, along with the MOBA category.
